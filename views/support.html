Frequently Asked Questions

How do I receive my product key after purchase?

After completing your purchase, your product key will be delivered instantly to your registered email address. You can also find all your purchased keys in your dashboard under "My Orders".

Are your software keys genuine?

Yes, all our software keys are 100% genuine and sourced directly from official suppliers. We guarantee their authenticity and provide full support for any activation issues.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and cryptocurrency payments. All payment methods are secure and encrypted.

What is your refund policy?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all software key purchases. If you encounter any issues with your key that our support team cannot resolve, we will issue a full refund.

How do I install the software after getting my key?

You'll need to download the software from the official website of the software provider. After installation, you'll be prompted to enter your product key during the activation process. Detailed installation guides are available in your order confirmation email.
